The Story

I'm an Enterprise Solution Architect based in Europe. Over 20+ years, my career has taken me through Oracle, Ericsson, and Vodafone/_VOIS — where I progressed from CRM analyst to Lead Solution Designer and Presales Service Architect — through to the European Commission, where I work as Enterprise Solution Architect today.

Along the way, I spent eight years as an independent consultant delivering CRM, BI, and ERP solutions, redesigning processes around Shared Service Center models, and mentoring junior consultants. I'm also a John Maxwell Certified Coach, Trainer, and Speaker — which means I bring a coaching mindset to every engagement: clarity, knowledge sharing, and empowering teams to sustain results independently.

Today, I focus on the intersection of data architecture, business process optimization, and AI-powered automation — helping organizations build solid data foundations, streamline workflows with tools like n8n and Claude, and adopt professional toolchains that make teams more productive.
