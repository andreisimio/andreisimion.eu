Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

This website (andreisimion.eu) is operated by Andrei Simion. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data Collection

This is a static website. By default, no personal data is collected, stored, or processed.

  • No cookies are set by this website (unless analytics are explicitly enabled).
  • No tracking pixels or third-party trackers are embedded.
  • Contact form submissions are sent directly via email or a configured form service, and are not stored on this server.

Analytics

If privacy-friendly analytics (such as Plausible Analytics) are enabled, they collect aggregate, anonymized data — no personal information, no cookies, and full GDPR compliance.

External Links

This site may link to external services (e.g., LinkedIn). These services have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by this website.

Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When you visit, your browser connects to Google servers. Google's privacy policy applies to this data transfer.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data. Since this site does not store personal data, these rights are already upheld by default.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact contact@andreisimion.eu.
